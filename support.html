Support

Answers to common questions about Streak Village: Daily Builder.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add to my village?

Open the app and tap "Check In" on any day you got something done — big or small. It doesn't matter what you accomplished, only that you showed up. Each check-in places a new building tile in your village.

What happens if I miss a day?

Nothing. Your village pauses and waits. No streak is reset, no punishment is applied. Come back the next day and keep building exactly where you left off. The village is patient.

What are the milestone buildings?

At a 7-day streak, a village fountain is placed. At 30 days, a library. At 100 days, a grand clock tower. Each milestone is a landmark that permanently marks your progress.

What do the villagers do?

Your stick-figure villagers react to your check-ins with waves, dances, and confetti. They celebrate your progress and are genuinely delighted to see you return, even after a long break.

Does the app work offline?

Yes, completely. Streak Village works with no internet connection. All village data stays on your device.

Does it collect any data?

No. 100% local. No accounts, no analytics, no ads.

Still need help?

email us at support@foculoom.com — we read every message and aim to respond within a few business days.
