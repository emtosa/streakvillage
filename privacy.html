Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Streak Village: Daily Builder ("the App") is designed from the ground up to respect your privacy. We collect no personal data whatsoever. This page explains exactly what that means.

What Data We Collect

None. Streak Village does not collect, transmit, store, or share any personal information, usage data, analytics, or crash reports that include identifiable information.

Where Your Data Lives

All village data — your check-ins, building placements, milestone progress, and settings — is stored exclusively in local storage on your device using Apple's standard iOS data storage APIs. This data never leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

Streak Village does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data-collection services. There are no SDKs from Facebook, Google, or any other advertising network included in the app.

Internet Connection

Streak Village does not require an internet connection and does not make any network requests during normal use. The app functions entirely offline.

Accounts

Streak Village requires no account, no sign-up, and no login. You do not need to provide an email address or any other identifying information to use the app.

Children's Privacy

Because we collect no data, Streak Village is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect information from children or any other users.

Changes to This Policy

If we ever change our privacy practices, we will update this page and revise the "Last updated" date above. Given our commitment to zero data collection, we do not anticipate meaningful changes.

Contact
